Oatmeal

Description

Morning oatmeal with dates, chia seeds, and cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Milk (dairy or otherwise)
  • Dates, chopped
  • Chia seeds
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt

    • Steps

    1. Add preferred amount of oats and dates to bowl.
    2. Add milk to cover oats.
    3. Microwave for about 2 minutes, or to desired consistency.
    4. Add toppings, including 1 tbsp of chia seeds, a pinch of salt, and plenty of cinnamon.
